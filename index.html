An Unforgettable Story

Al Ahly SC was founded on April 24, 1907 by a group of patriotic Egyptian youth as a symbol of resistance and national pride against British occupation.

From the streets of Cairo to the summit of Africa, Al Ahly has maintained its status as the most decorated club on the continent and the undisputed Club of the African Century.

The colour red was never just a colour — it is the blood, sweat, and pride of generations of fans and athletes who carried the badge with honour.
